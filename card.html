University student with ADHD and anxiety

Aurora is a 19-year-old university student who, in addition to her studies, plays sports during extracurricular hours and works part-time.

The course Aurora attends requires her to do several reading and research assignments, however, these activities become difficult as she has difficulty concentrating on reading long text.

To help her with her reading, Aurora uses a software program that highlights each paragraph of text where she has her cursor so that she can concentrate on one small portion of the text at a time.

Although this is not always the case, she has a preference for minimalist websites with only one column and little information, where the only color is in the images that help interpret the text.

To better manage her time and tasks, Aurora uses the Trello tool where she can list everything she needs to do through checklists, which ultimately helps with anxiety control.
